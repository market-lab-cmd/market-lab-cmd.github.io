# Skill Game Decision Making

Senior-player review angle: A good review separates decision quality from outcome. A good result can hide a weak decision, and a bad result can follow a reasonable decision.

Key questions:

- What information was visible before acting?
- What real options were available?
- Did the risk have a job?
- Would the same decision be worth repeating in a similar situation?
