# Game Strategy Notes Hub

This page is the root review desk for the game strategy notes network.

The writing should stay in a senior-player voice: calm, observant, and focused on decision quality. It should describe how a player reviews a real session, not how a product is promoted.

## Main Review Angle

- What decision changed the game?
- Was the risk useful, forced, avoidable, or emotional?
- Which table signal was missed?
- Which mistake has appeared before?
- What should be watched in the next session?

## Tone Rules

- Use review language: decision, risk, timing, awareness, pattern, mistake, session.
- Avoid marketing language: guaranteed wins, best platform, ultimate tricks, promotional claims.
- Keep the content educational and neutral.
