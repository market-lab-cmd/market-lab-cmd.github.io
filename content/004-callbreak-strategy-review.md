# Callbreak Strategy Review

Senior-player review angle: Callbreak should be reviewed from the call outward. The call should describe the hand, not the player's hope.

Key questions:

- Which tricks were reliable, conditional, or dependent on opponents making mistakes?
- Did suit control support the call?
- Was trump timing used to protect the plan or spent too early?
- Did the player remember which cards were no longer available?
